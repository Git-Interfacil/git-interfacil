Frequently Asked Questions
Q: Why isn't my folder showing up after I select it?
A: You must select a folder that is a git repository.
Q: What actions can I perform in this program?
A: In this release, you can commit, push, stash, pop, and pull.
Q: Where's the add button?
A: When you select the files and press commit, it automatically adds these files and commits them.
Q: How can I confirm if the actions worked without checking in the terminal?
A: Every time you perform an action, a toast notification appears on the screen.
